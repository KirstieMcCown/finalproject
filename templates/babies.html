The total number of babies born to mothers in Australia, between 2010 and 2018 was

47,552,179

Thats a whopping 70% more babies than mothers! Hover over each bar on the chart to the left, to see the exact number of babies born each year. Select the next button below, to move to the next visualisation.

The number of singleton births makes up almost

98%


of births in Australia!
The remaining 2% are from either a twin or multiple birth! So if you are a twin, know a twin or have a twins in your family; count yourself pretty lucky as they're a rare find!

Between 2010 and 2018, the state with the highest number of babies born is

New South Wales

THis follows along with the same trend seen in mothers for this time period! In 2018, the number of babies in Australia grew, by almost 50% also!
